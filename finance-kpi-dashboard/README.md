# Sales Decision Simulator

## Project Overview
This project simulates various sales strategies and models business outcomes to support data-driven decision-making. It uses historical sales data to predict results under different scenarios, helping teams optimize strategies and improve revenue performance.

## Objectives
- Simulate sales strategies and predict outcomes.  
- Identify high-impact decisions for revenue growth.  
- Provide insights into sales performance patterns.

## Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data aggregation and analysis.  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and visualizations.  
- **Excel / CSV** – Sample datasets for simulation.
